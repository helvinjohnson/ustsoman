Services



IP TV

IP TV is a digital television service that is delivered over the internet protocol (IP) networks, allowing viewers to stream and watch TV programs on various devices.

SMA TV

SMA TV stands for "Social Media Advertising Television," which refers to advertising campaigns that use social media platforms to deliver targeted ads to TV viewers.

MA TV

MA TV stands for "Marketing Automation Television," which involves using data and technology to automate the process of delivering targeted marketing messages to TV viewers.

HOME AUTOMATION

Home automation involves using technology to control and automate various devices and systems in a home, such as lighting, temperature, security, and entertainment systems.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Web development involves creating, designing, and maintaining websites for various purposes, such as personal blogs, business sites, e-commerce platforms, and online applications.

SERVALANCE SYSTEM

Surveillance systems are security systems that use cameras and other devices to monitor and record activity in a given area, often for the purpose of preventing crime or ensuring safety.
